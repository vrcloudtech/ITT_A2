What does it do?

Cyber security is technology which prevents data access to those who are not prohibited to view such data, such as database information,code or need to know information for government agencies which may be a security risk if exposed.

At the current time even the most state of the art cyber security is exploitable with enough man power and time however,to maintain top cybersecurity for technologically stored data cyber security practices should be implemented where possible, cyber security practices may include but are not limited to things as basic as ensuring that your are using an antivirus and firewall to things more complex such as penetration testing,encryption and user verification procedures which may include verifications texts or account login emails.

As of 22 of february 2018 the australian government implemented a NDB(Notifiable Data Breach) Scheme which obligates organisations to notify individuals of potential data breaches and conduct assessment of any data breaches or suspected data breaches which may cause harm due to data breach or leakage, the notification off an organisation involved in the data breach must also provide recommendations to avoid further damage due to the data breach, more information in relation to this scheme can be found here https://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy-law/privacy-act/notifiable-data-breaches-scheme#overview.

The NDB Scheme in itself is a form of cyber security although this scheme does also provide incentive for organisations and companies to maintain good cyber security practices in order to prevent a negative image on the company as even the best cyber security practices will simply delay data breaches however the data store with good security practices will highly discourage ‘hackers’ or potential Problems for stored data to ‘not be worth their time’ as for very Good cyber security practices breaching this data could potentially take years and if the cyber security practices are constantly updated over time the process of breaching this data will take even longer.
